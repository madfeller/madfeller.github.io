There is no denying the devastating toll humanity has had on our natural world as we have progressed over the past 200 years. Thankfully, governments around the world have begun to commit to action - including the commonwealth of Virginia with the passage of the Virginia Clean Economy Act in 2020.

We've committed to action, but we do not yet know the best path forward.

Join the Midguard as we explore the questions surrounding Virginia's clean energy transition and ask ourselves - is this how we produce the most useful energy for each acre we sacrifice from nature?
